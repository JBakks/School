My favourite spots for others to discover on campus are:

Taylor Library

Taylor Library is the best place to study! I like how it's not completely silent, but still not loud enough to get my work done. I enjoy hanging out with friends cramming in our last couple of hours of studying!

The Spoke

The Spoke is one of the best places to sit back and relax after a hard day of work. I enjoy the music they play (except when they play it too loud). The buffalo fingers are my go-to!

 

Sommerville House

If you haven't been to the renovation of Sommerville House, I recommend it. They have a huge study space, with great conference rooms to meet with groups. The food there is a plus as well!
