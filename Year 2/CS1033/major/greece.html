Greece

Food

It is said that Greeks are masters of grilled and spit-roasted meats, so that would be my first food to try after touching down in Greece.

Souvlaki is chunks of pork served on chopped tomatoes and onions in pita bread with tzatziki. Look how great it looks!

Activities

I feel almost as if it is a must to go swimming in the crystal water in Greece. In the summer the average temperature is 29°C so you would for sure need a way to cool down, and what better way then swimming!

Fun Facts

  • Greece is made up of thousands of islands
  • 80% of Greece is made up up of mountains
  • 240 species of birds
  • Greece is the third-largest producer of olives
