Ember Candles


Ember is a business that specializes in creating and selling handmade candles. The company uses high-quality materials and unique fragrances to create a range of candles for different occasions and preferences. The candles are hand-poured and carefully crafted by skilled artisans to ensure the best quality and attention to detail. The company offers a variety of candle types, including soy, beeswax, and scented candles, as well as accessories such as candle holders and gift sets. The candles are sold online and in select retail stores, and the company also offers custom orders and bulk discounts for customers.
